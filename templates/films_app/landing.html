About Your Cinema Films

Your Cinema Films is a platform that bridges the gap between audiences and cinema programming. We believe that moviegoers should have a say in what films are shown on the big screen.

For classic films, you can vote for up to 10 films that you'd like to see return to theaters. For current and upcoming releases, you can vote for up to 3 films you're most excited about. Our cinema partners use this data to make programming decisions.

Yes, you need to create a free account to vote for films. This helps us prevent duplicate voting and allows you to manage your votes over time. You can easily sign up using your email or Google account.

Our classic film collection includes a curated selection of significant and beloved films from cinema history. Current and upcoming releases are sourced from official UK cinema release schedules. If you'd like to suggest a film that's not in our database, please contact us.

Yes, you can change your votes at any time. Simply go to your profile page to see your current votes, and you can remove votes for films you're no longer interested in to make room for new selections.

We regularly update our Current & Upcoming section with the latest cinema releases. For information about special screenings of classic films based on user votes, check the dashboard for announcements or follow us on social media.
